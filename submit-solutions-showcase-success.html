Thanks! Your application for the CEE Solutions Showcase has been received. Here are details about the rest of the process:

Within 24 hours, we will notify you if the Application will move forward to the Showcase and Awards. These are next steps (assuming that the application is accepted):

  • All Jury Members will receive your Text application (along with the other applications) before 8 January, for their review.
  • On 13 January (14:30-16:30 CET), you will present to the Jury. You will have 12 minutes to present your CaseStudy, followed by 3 min Q&A. (Our Hopin support team will contact you 4-5 days in advance to check all technical issues (audio, video, screen-share, etc.)).
  • Your application will be automatically included in the Awards competition. (The Awards follows immediately after the daytime Summit.)
  • Danuta from our office will issue you invoice in next 1-2 days. The fee also includes:
    • 2 Tickets to attend the full event.
    • Digital Exhibition Booth. For your digital Exhibition Booth, we will need a YouTube Video Link (Here is example of what information booth looks like.)
    • Full Page Advertisement in the Event Brochure (PDF emailed to all attendees, just prior to the event). Please prepare format for A5.
