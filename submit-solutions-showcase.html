CEE Solutions Showcase - Submit your Application

A new way to present your digital solution on stage to the full audience and Jury: 15 minutes case-study of a digital solution to a business problem. Limited to just 9 companies, all Showcases are automatically submitted to the Awards.

Here are details about the “CEE Solutions Showcase”:

  • limited to 9 pre-approved companies in subject-exclusive categories.
  • 15 minutes case-study Presentation of a digital solution to a business problem. Topic must be approved by the organizer (and each of the 9 slots is topic-exclusive). You will present in front of the Jury on 13 January.
  • Automatic Inclusion in the Awards competition, with Winners to be announced the evening of 13 January at the online CEE Digital Services Awards.

Additional benefits of submitting your "Solutions Showcase":

  • Logos on Website and Hopin platform
  • Logos on all Email and Social Media promotions
  • 1750 euro fee includes:
    • 2 Tickets to attend the Virtual Summit.
    • Static Booth with pre-loaded Video-streaming and supporting information.
    • Full Page Advertisement in the Event Brochure (PDF emailed to all attendees, just prior to the event).
  • Your full contact details shared with VIP Jury members.

While all CEE Solutions Showcases will present "live" in front of our Jury on 13 January, all Applications must be submitted via the form below. Pre-approvals to move forward will be announced within 1 day of submitting your Application. We are looking for outstanding examples of digital solutions that solved real business problems. The best Applications will be world-class, unique, compelling, and demonstrate strong ROI.
